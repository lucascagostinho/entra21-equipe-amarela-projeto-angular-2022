About

This site is an innovative social network that aims to unite athletes in a place where they can exchange experiences and also encourage people to practice some sport.

The team is composed by the members Alexsandro Corrêa, Ciro Ugalde, Stephanie Vieira, Lucas Corrêa Agostinho and brings as an application, the idea of ​​a Social Network for the practice of sports, which provides the possibility of creating an account, controlling the profile, interacting with other users and recording their activities. The project is currently in its version of the application of the concepts learned so far in the course.

The project is under development and will initially have a website and a functional desktop application. More updates will come in the future.
